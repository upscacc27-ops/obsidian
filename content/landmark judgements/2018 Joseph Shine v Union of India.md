### Supreme Court judgment
- decriminalized adultery - struck down Section 497 of IPC, on grounds of being unconstitutional & being violative of Art 14, 15, 21
- declared Section 198(2) unconstitutional to the extent that it is applicable to adultery
- **Impact** - overruled all judgements upholding Section 497 of IPC, adultery is no longer a criminal offence, however it is a civil wrong & grounds for dissolution of marriage
