### SC Judgement
- issued 7 directives to central, state & UT governments/ administrations related to police reforms
	1. Constitution of a State Security commission
	2. Selection of DGP from 3 senior-most officers empanelled by UPSC for the post, minimum tenure of 2 years
	3. minimum tenure of 2 years for police officers on operational duties
	4. separation of investigative police from law & order police
	5. set up a Police Establishment Board
	6. set up a Police Complaints Authority @ State & District levels
	7. set up a National Security Commission at central level
- **Impact** - led to police reforms, however partially. could not eliminate political interference in functioning, especially postings & transfers. State security commissions & Police Establishment Boards have been setup, but lack authority to make binding recommendations.
