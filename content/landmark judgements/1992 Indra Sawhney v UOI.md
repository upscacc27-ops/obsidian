### SC judgement
- upheld validity of OBC reservation of 27% under Art 16(4), with conditions like creamy layer, no reservation in promotions, capped at 50%, ceiling of 50% for backlog vacancies
- invalidated 10% additional reservation for other EWS, not already covered 
- **Impact** - Appointment of Ram Nandan Committee to identify Creamy Layer, Est. of NCBC to examine exclusion parameters, enactment of 5 CAAs down the line
	1. 76th CAA, 1994 - placed *TN Reservation Act* in 9th Schedule
	2. 77th CAA, 1995 - nullified ruling wrt reservation in promotion
	3. 81st CAA, 2000 - nullified ruling wrt backlog vacancies
	4. 82nd CAA, 2000 - provides relaxation in parameters - facilitating reservation in promotion
	5. 85th CAA, 2001 - consequential seniority to SC, ST in promotion
