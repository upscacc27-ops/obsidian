### Supreme Court Judgement
- Women of all ages can enter & worship Lord Ayyappa
- declared ban on entry of women in age group 10-50 unconstitutional
- ban violates Art 14, 21 & 25
- struck down Rule 3(b) of Kerala Hindu Places of public worship Rules, 1965
- **Impact** - Protests & Hartals by devotees in Kerala, some women attempted to enter but faced resistance from devotees, priests & authorities
