### Supreme Court judgement
- invalidated Clause(4) & (5) of Art 368 which were inserted by [[42nd CAA 1976]], similarly amendment made to [[Art 31C Saving of laws giving effect to cer tain DPSPs]] by 42nd CAA was held unconstitutional on grounds of being violative of Basic Structure
- held limited amending power of the Parliament, judicial review, harmony & balance between FRs & DPSPs are the basic features of the Constitution
- **Impact** - nullified the attempt to dilute the doctrine of the basic structure by 42nd CAA, SC held that Parliament cannot under Art 368 expand its amending power or acquire the right to repeal or abrogate the constitution or destroy its basic features.  
- SC held that anything that destroys the balance between FRs & DPSPs will destroy an essential element of the basic structure of the constitution
