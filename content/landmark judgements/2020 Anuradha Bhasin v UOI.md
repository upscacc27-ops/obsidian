### SC judgement
- Freedom of speech & expression, carrying on trade, occupation over the internet enjoys constitutional protection under [[Art 19(1)(a) Freedom of Speech or Expression]] & [[Art 19(1)(g) Freedom of profession]]
- An order suspending internet services indefinitely is impermissible under the Temporary Suspension of Telecom Services Rules, 2017
- Any such suspension order must adhere to principle of proportionality and not extend beyond necessary duration, not more than 15 days
- All such orders must be made public & are subject to Judicial Review
- **Impact** - Emergence of Right to access internet under [[Art 19(1)(g) Freedom of profession]] as a fundamental right. Also recognition of right to proportional shutdowns.
