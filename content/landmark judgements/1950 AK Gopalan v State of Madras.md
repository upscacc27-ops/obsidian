### Supreme Court judgement
- invalidated section 14 of impugned Preventive Detention Act 1950 on grounds of violation of [[Art 22 Protection against arrest and detention]]
- omission of this section will not change the objective of the legislation
- Personal liberty under [[Art 21 Right to life and personal liberty]] means liberty of the physical body i.e. freedom of physical restraints
- Art 21 is protection only against arbitrary Executive action not arbitrary Legislation
- **Impact** - 
	- SC took narrow interpretation of Art 21
	- held that term "law" used in Art 21 means state made law, not principle of natural justice
	- Procedure established by Law is different from Due process of Law
