### Supreme Court judgement
- held that sexual harassment of women at workplace is violative of Art 15 & Art 21
- duty of employer or responsible person in any institution - public or private to prevent such harassment
- gave detailed guidelines, to be strictly observed by all employees until a suitable legislation is enacted
- **Impact** - Vishaka Guidelines filled the legislative vacuum in this subject. Later Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition & Redressal) Act, 2013 was enacted.
