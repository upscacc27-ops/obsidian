### SC judgement
- Between 1967-81, Governor of Bihar re-promulgated 256 ordinances, kept in force for 1-14 years
- This exceptional power != substitute to law making in legislature

> [!NOTE] Keywords
> **Subversion of the democratic process** and **Fraud on the Constitution**
> Can't be **Perverted to meet political ends**

- **Impact** : declared Bihar Intermediate Education Council Ordinance, 1985 unconstitutional, exposed that the ordinance making power can be misused
