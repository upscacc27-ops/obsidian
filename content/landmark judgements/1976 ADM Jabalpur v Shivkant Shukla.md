### Supreme Court Judgement
- Art 21 is sole repository of right to personal liberty against the state
- if Art 359 suspends its enforcement, then detenu will have no locus standi to a writ petition, challenging legality of the detention
- Court held [[Art 21 Right to life and personal liberty]] could be suspended during emergency
- Court upheld the constitutional validity of Section 16A(9) of Maintenance of internal security Act, 1971
- Seen as a low point in Indian judicial history & was widely criticized for undermining civil liberties
- Justice HR Khanna famously dissented, saying right to life is inherent & inalienable
