### SC Judgement
- Active euthanasia is not permissible, passive is permissible with certain conditions, safeguards & protocols 
	1. Decision has to be taken by parents/ spouse/ close relative/ or in their absence, next friend/ doctors treating
	2. Even if decision is taken by close relative, friend or doctors - require HC approval
	3. On filing of such an application, CJ of HC must constitute at least 2 judge bench who decide whether approval should be granted - on advice of committee of 3 reputed doctors
	4. HC Bench should issue notice to all related, with a copy of doctors committee's report
	5. The HC must adjudicate after hearing them
