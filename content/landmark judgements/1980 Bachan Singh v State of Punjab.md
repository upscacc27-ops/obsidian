### Supreme Court judgement
- death penalty aka capital punishment in Section 302 of IPC as an alternative punishment for offence of murder, is not unreasonable
- It does not violate ethos of Art 19 & Art 21 
- Art 21 recognizes the right of State to deprive a person of life & liberty, in accordance with a procedure being fair, just & reasonable
- Death penalty is an exception to a general rule of life imprisonment, must be confined to the rarest of the rare cases
- exceptional & grave circumstances of the crime & criminal must be considered
- **Impact** : led to [[Doctrine of rarest of the rare]], affected various subsequent cases
