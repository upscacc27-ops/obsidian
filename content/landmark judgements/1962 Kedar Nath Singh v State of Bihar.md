### Supreme Court Judgement
- Upheld the constitutional validity of Section 124A, ruling it as a reasonable restrictions under Art 19(2) that assists [[Art 19(1)(a) Freedom of Speech or Expression]]
- Narrow interpretation by SC to avoid the law being struck down
- every citizen has the right to criticize or comment on the govt- verbal or written to an extent that it does not incite violence or creates public disorder
- Dissent & Criticism are essential for a healthy democracy
