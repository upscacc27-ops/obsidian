### Supreme Court judgements
- decriminalized homosexuality & partly struck down Section 377 of IPC - that criminalized carnal intercourse as against the order of nature
- held that this section is unconstitutional & as it criminalizes a consensual act of adults
- violates Art 14, Art 15, Art 19(1)(a) & Art 21
- **Impact**: overruled earlier judgement in [[2013 Suresh Kumar Koushal case]], LGBT now legally permitted to engage in consensual sexual conduct
