### Supreme Court judgment
- reaffirmed the applicability of doctrine of basic structure
- struck down clause (4) of Art 329A that was added by [[39th CAA 1975]], this provision had kept election disputes involving the Prime Minister & Speaker of Lok Sabha outside the jurisdiction of all courts
- Supreme court held that such a provision is beyond amending power of the Parliament & affected the Basic Structure
- **Impact** - following [[1973 Keshavananda Bharti v UOI]] & this judgement, [[42nd CAA 1976]] inserted 2 new clauses (4) & (5) in [[Art 368 Parliament's power to amend the Constitution and procedures thereafter]]
	- Clause (4) provided that amendments included to FRs, made under Art 368 cannot be questioned in any court on any ground
	- Clause (5) - there should be no limitation whatsoever to constituent power of Parliament under Art 368
