### Supreme Court judgement
- overruled its previous judgement in SP Gupta v UOI, aka First Judges Case.
- held that judges of SC & HC shall be appointed by the President on advice of CJI. CJI shall advise after taking advice from 2 of his senior most colleagues
- Court has challenged the meaning of above consultation to that of concurrence
- also held that the senior most judge should be appointed CJI
- **Impact** - Consequent to this & also the Advisory Opinion tendered in the Third Judges Case, a Memorandum of Procedure for appointment of judges to the SC & HCs was formulated.
- This procedure invented by Judiciary is called Collegium system
