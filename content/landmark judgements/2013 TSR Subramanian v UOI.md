### Supreme Court judgement
- issued various directions to central, state & UT govts. for bringing civil services reforms
	1. Constitution of Civil Services Boards to guide the govt. on transfers, postings, disciplinary actions etc.
	2. Providing a fixed minimum tenure to civil servants
	3. mandating that civil servants must not act on the basis of oral instructions, orders, suggestions, proposals or directions, should be in writing or formally recorded if oral
- **Impact** - led to amendments in Rule 7 of IAS, IPS, IFoS Cadre rules vide a notification issued in 2014. The respective Cadre Controlling Authorities have been directed to implement above directives, however State Civil Service Boards still not functioning effectively
