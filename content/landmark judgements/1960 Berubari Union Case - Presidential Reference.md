### Supreme Court judgement
- Ruled that the power of Parliament under Art 3 does not include cession of Indian territory
- Indian territory can only be ceded by a CAA under Art 368.
- Preamble is **not** a part of the Constitution
- **Impact** - led to enactment of [[9th CAA 1960]] to transfer certain territory known as Berubari Union to Pakistan. This transfer was done as a part of Nehru-Noon Agreement of 1958.
