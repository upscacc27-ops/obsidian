### Supreme Court Judgement
- held that power under [[Art 32 SC's Writ Jurisdiction]] is not only injective i.e. preventing the infringement of FRs, but also remedial in scope & providing relief against the violation already committed
- clean air & environment are essential for life & health
- relief includes power to provide compensation
- infringement/ violation of FR must be gross/patent i.e. obvious (incontrovertible)
- **Impact** - led to expansion of ambit of Environmental Law in the country, introduced principle of absolute liability in place of strict liability, introduced precautionary principle & sustainable development doctrine
