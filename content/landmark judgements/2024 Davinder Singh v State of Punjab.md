### Supreme Court Judgement

- **E.V. Chinnaiah v. State of Andhra Pradesh** had held SC = homogeneous class - cannot be sub classified
- Reviewed validity of above case - and held sub-classification is constitutionally permissible under Art 16(4)
- Better targeting of the most disadvantaged
