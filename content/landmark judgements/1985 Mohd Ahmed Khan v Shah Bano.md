### SC Judgement
- upheld the judgement by MP HC, Muslim husband is liable to pay maintenance to divorced wife beyond the period of iddat. 
- Held Section 125 of CrPC is secular & applies to all religions
- This section will prevail over personal laws incase of conflict
- UCC will help cause of national integration by removing disparate loyalties to conflicting laws
- **Impact** - Enactment of new law - Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Divorce), 1986
