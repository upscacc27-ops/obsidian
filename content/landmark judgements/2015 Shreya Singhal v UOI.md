### SC judgement
- struck down Section 66A of the IT Act, 2000,  declaring it unconstitutional and violative of FRs
- language was vague & overbroad, no clear guidelines, terms were subjective & arbitrary - prone to misuse by authorities
- imposed a **Chilling effect** on free speech
- [[Art 19(1)(a) Freedom of Speech or Expression]] cannot be curtailed unless by reasonable restrictions outline in Art 19(2)
