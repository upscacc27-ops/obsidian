### Supreme Court Judgement
- statutory status must be conferred upon Central Vigilance Commission (CVC)
- make CBI as autonomous body for efficient & effective functioning as a non-partisan agency
- similar instructions for ED
- struck down a provision of Single Directive that required CBI to obtain prior permission from the Central Govt. before conducting an investigation against the officers of the rank of Joint Secretary and above
- **Impact** - enactment of CVC Act, 2003, superintendence of CBI as far as it relates to investigation related to Corruption is vested in CVC. This act also reinstated the Single Directive Provision, which was again struck down in 2014, being violative of Art 14
