### Supreme Court judgement
- declared triple-talaq as unconstitutional
- held that it is arbitrary as in the sense that the marital tie can be whimsically broken down by a Muslim man without any reconciliation to save it
- found it violative of Art 14, declared Section 2 of Muslim Personal(Shariat) Law Application Act, 1937 void. - it recognized & enforced triple talaq
- held triple talaq is against the tenets of Holy Quran. 
- directed the Central govt. to make law governing Muslim divorce within 6 months
- **Impact** - Enactment of Muslim Women(Protection of Rights on Marriage) Act 2019
