### Supreme Court Judgment
- upheld 77th, 81st, 82nd, 85th CAA - see [[1992 Indra Sawhney v UOI]]
- impugned above CAAs by which articles 16(4A) and 16(4B) have been inserted, flow from from Art 16(4), and do not alter its structure
- they retain the factors like backwardness & inadequate representation for State to provide reservation
- the impugned CAAs do not obliterate any of existing constitutional constructs - 50% cap, OBC-NCL
- 3 qualifiers before providing reservation to SC/ST in public employment
	1. Quantifiable data showing backwardness
	2. Quantifiable data showing inadequate representation
	3. should not affect administrative efficiency under [[Art 335 Claims of SCs and STs to posts]]
