### SC judgment
- President Rule according to Art 356 in Madhya P, Himachal P, Rajasthan = valid in 1992
- Invalid in Nagaland(1988), Karnataka(1989), Meghalaya(1991)
- New propositions
	- validity of proclamation open to Judicial Review
	- Union Govt's responsibility to ensure satisfying material exists for proclamation
	- [[Art 74 COM's advice to President]] does not bar scrutiny of the above material
	- notwithstanding that it is passed in both houses, if invalid, open to restoration of State Leg Assembly and Govt
	- Secularism = part of Basic St. and anti-secularism can be considered a ground for proclamation
- **Impact** : Check on arbitrary use of Art 356
