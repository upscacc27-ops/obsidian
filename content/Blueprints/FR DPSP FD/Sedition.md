### Way Forward
- Clear definition - Amend Section 152 of BNS to explicitly state *Incitement to violence is mandatory prerequisite to sedition* 
- Address **Process as Punishment** issues - implement Law Commission's recommendations - preliminary inquiry to verify if there was an incitement to violence before filing FIR, mandatory sanction from govt before prosecution
- Increased Judicial oversight - rigorously evaluate Sedition cases
- Recourse for victims - compensation for falsely accused and maliciously prosecuted
- Focus on root cause - mitigate extreme sentiments

### Conclusion
- Every irresponsible exercise of free speech cannot be termed seditious, protection of national integrity cannot be used as a tool to curb individual liberty
- Dissent & Criticism are part of debate on policy issues and strengthen democracy
- Every restriction on free speech must be carefully scrutinized to avoid unwanted restrictions
