### Context
Uttarakhand govt. passed UCC Act 2024. This makes important changes in areas such as marriages, divorce & succession.

### Introduction
UCC is a proposition to replace the personal laws based on scriptures & religious customs with a common set of laws governing every citizen. These laws include personal matters like marriage, divorce, inheritance, adoption & maintenance

### Constitutional Basis
[[Art 44 UCC for citizens]]

### Status in India
- need was debated in the constitutional assembly, decided to put under DPSP, since the adoption of constitution, govt. has made certain efforts towards its implementation, like
1. Enactment of Special Marriage Act, 1954 - secular alternative in marriages, inheritance rights of off-springs from such marriages were to be governed by religion-neutral chapter on inheritance
2. Continued application of Uniform civil laws in Goa & Puducherry - Parliamentary law was enacted to continue Portuguese civil code of 1867 in Goa, Renoncants in Puducherry are still governed by 218-year old French Civil Code of 1804
3. Uttarakhand UCC - tabled a bill
4. Nationwide UCC is still a far fetched dream

### Conclusion
UCC in its true spirit, must be brought by making gradual changes. Focus should first be on eradication of discriminatory practices against women, rather than uniformity. 
However, until that is done, small reforms, correcting inherent irrationalities, & adapting old customs to contemporary needs will lay the foundation of nationwide UCC
