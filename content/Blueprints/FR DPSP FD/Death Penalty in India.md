- Scandinavian countries like Sweden, Norway, Finland have lowest crime rates without any such rule, focus is on reformation rather than retribution
- As of 2026, majority of countries have abolished it in law or practice, India remains in a shrinking minority
- Law Commission Report, 2015 - abolition of death penalty for all offences except terrorism, waging war against state

### Conclusion
Law Commission in 2015 report proposed abolition, Scandinavian countries also an example
