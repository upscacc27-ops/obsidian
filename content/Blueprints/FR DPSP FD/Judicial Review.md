
### Introduction
- Judiciary's power to examine constitutionality of legislative enactments and executive actions, ensures all organs work within the constitutional limits
- India follows Checks & Balances, JR checks Leg & Exec
- [[Art 32 SC's Writ Jurisdiction]] = Heart & Soul of the Constitution - *Dr. Ambedkar*

### Evolution
- USA - Marbury v Madison, no explicit mention, SC assumed the JR power by interpretation
- India - SC declared JR part of Basic St.
