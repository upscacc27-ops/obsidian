### Introduction
[[Art 21 Right to life and personal liberty]] , initially interpreted narrowly, it has undergone a transformative judicial expansion
SC has interpreted "life" to mean not merely animal existence, but a life with dignity, autonomy, privacy & meaningful existence

### Conclusion
- Art 21 has evolved from a narrow procedural guarantee to heart of the Fundamental Rights, through judicial interpretation, now protects dignity, privacy, autonomy, livelihood & environment & climate security.
- Expansion reflect Transformative Constitutionalism & commitment to dignified & meaningful life
