### Context
Karnataka govt. withheld *Karnataka State Employment of Local Candidates in Industries, Factories & Other Establishments Bill 2024* because of stiff resistance from private industries

### Historical evidences

| Year | Development                                                                                                 |
| ---- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2008 | Maharashtra attempted to provide 80% reservation to sons of the soil                                        |
| 2019 | Andhra P. passed a law mandating 75% local reservation                                                      |
| 2020 | Haryana govt. provided for 75% reservation in private sector - held unconstitutional by Punjab & Haryana HC |
| 2024 | Karnataka govt - 50% reservation in managerial positions & 70% in non-manage.                               |
### Arguments

| Arguments in support                                                                                         | Arguments against                                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Protection of right to livelihood for domiciled individuals                                                  | decreased inter-state mobility impacts national economy                            |
| Art 16(4) - constitutional mandate                                                                           | Restricted employment choices for companies decreases competitiveness              |
| Tackling rising unemployment - Haryana highest (9%) as of 2021-22                                            | Hampers inclusive growth of migrants from underdeveloped states, increase HDI gap  |
| Resentment against migrant workers                                                                           | threat to national unity - antagonistic regionalism - Sons of soil - Social fabric |
| Private sector utilizes public sector - Right of the state                                                   | against 50% cap                                                                    |
| Number of private sector jobs >> public sector - better & faster substantive equality                        | majority of MSMEs depend on migrant labour                                         |
| US Civil Rights Act of 1964, Canada - The Employment Equity Act - both provide reservation in private sector |                                                                                    |

### Way forward
- Uphold uniform labor rights for both migrants & locals - prevent exploitation
- Complement with Reforms - Easy of Doing business, Skill development, infra development
- Supreme Court must lay down uniform guidelines
- *Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat* promote inter-state cultural interactions
- Greater consultation & Deliberation with all stakeholders

### Conclusion
aims to promote regional employment & address local aspirations
must balance efficiency with equality and mobility, calibrated & incentive based approach essential for inclusive growth & healthy business environment
