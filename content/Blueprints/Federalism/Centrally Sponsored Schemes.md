### Introduction
Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) are schemes formulated by the Union govt. but implemented by State govts., with funding shared between Centre & states in predefined ratios
Represent an instrument through which the Union influences policy in areas that often fall within concurrent & state list, directly impacting India's fiscal federal structure

CSS are distinct from Central Sector Schemes & Finance Commission Transfers

### Conclusion
CSS represent a powerful fiscal instrument through which the Union shapes national development priorities, long term sustainability of CSS depends on
- balancing national standards with state level flexibility
- strengthening institutional consultation
- move from control-based funding to trust-based relationship
