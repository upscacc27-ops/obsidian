### Introduction
Inter State council = constitutional body design to foster cooperation between Union & State governments
Vital forum that promotes cooperative federalism, facilitates dialogue over litigation
Council may meet at least thrice a year

### Background
- [[Art 263 Provisions wrt Inter State Council]] - empowers President to establish such Council
- Despite this provision existing since adoption, it was established in 1990 by VP Singh govt following recommendations of [[Sarkaria Commission, 1988]]

### Composition
- Chairman - Prime Minister
- Members  - CMs of all states & UTs with legislatures, Administrators of UTs without legislatures, 6 members of Cabinet Rank - nominated by PM
- Permanent invitees - 13 Union ministers are permanent invitees

### Functions & Duties
- Coordination between Centre & states
- Investigation & discussion on subjects of common interest
- Inquire, advise on & resolve disputes
- make recommendations for policy & action

### Standing committee of ISC
- to ensure continuous dialogue, council has a standing committee, established in 1996
- has been reconstituted time to time with the approval of Chairman, last reconstituted 2024
- Composition of standing committee - Home minister + 5 Union Ministers + 9 CMs
- role is to facilitate consultation & deliberation before issues are taken up by full council
- also oversees implementation of decisions taken
- is assisted by a secretariat called Inter State Council Secretariat, setup in 1991 headed by secretary to govt of India, also functioning as a secretariat to Zonal councils since 2011
