### Introduction
Indian Federalism is a *holding together* federalism where political, institutional & financial developments have introduced competition between states

Competitive federalism thus, refers to competition between states to improve governance, attract investment & promote development

### Drivers & Mechanisms promoting competition
- Liberalization - increased states' economic autonomy, Globalization increased competition for foreign investments
- Rise of regional political parties strengthened state autonomy
- Investment Summits
- Ease of Doing Business rankings, NITI Ayog indices
- 16th FC adding GDP as a criterion to decide share in devolution
