### Context

> [!quote] Keyword
> Executive Fiat to issue Ordinances

Since 2014, 11 ordinances per year, compared to 6.1 the previous decade

### Introduction
power to issue an ordinance is an emergency legislative power given to executive when Parliament & State Legislature is not in session

Re-promulgation means issuing the same ordinance again & again after it lapses, without placing it before the legislature bypassing opposition scrutiny

### Constitutional Provisions
- [[Art 123 President's ordinance making power]] 
	- use - Parliament is not in session (both houses), or when President is satisfied that immediate action is necessary
	- effect - same force & effect as an Act passed by Parliament, can create a new law, amend or repeal
	- time limit - must be placed before Parliament when it reassembles, automatically lapse after 6 weeks from date Parliament reconvenes, unless approved by both houses, or if both houses disapprove, lapses immediately
