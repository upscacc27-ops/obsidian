- Undermines – undermines constitutional values.

- Erodes – erodes public trust in institutions.

- Weakens – weakens democratic accountability.

- Distorts – distorts electoral processes.

- Aggravates (aggravates = makes a problem worse) – aggravates social tensions.

- Fuels – fuels communal polarisation.

- Threatens – threatens institutional autonomy.

- Marginalises – marginalises vulnerable communities.

- Dilutes – dilutes the spirit of constitutional provisions.

- Compromises – compromises transparency in governance.

- Subverts (subvert = secretly weaken or overthrow) – subverts democratic institutions.

- Entrenches (entrench = make something deeply rooted and hard to remove) – entrenches social inequality.

- Exacerbates (exacerbate = intensify a problem) – exacerbates regional disparities.

- Curtails (curtail = reduce or restrict) – curtails civil liberties.

- Jeopardises (jeopardise = put something at risk) – jeopardises national unity.
